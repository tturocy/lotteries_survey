To select the option you prefer, click on the picture of the corresponding horizontal bar. When you do, that option will be highlighted on your screen. If you change your mind, you can click the other option instead; that option will then be highlighted. You can revise your choice as often as you would like. When you are satisfied with your choice, click the button labeled Confirm decision, which will record your decision and move you to the next decision round.

Use the display above to practice selecting options by clicking on them. When you are ready to move to the next instruction screen, click on the Confirm decision button.
